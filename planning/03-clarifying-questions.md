# Clarifying Questions Before Build

## Product Direction
1. Is the primary launch audience high school students only, or also career switchers from day one?
2. Should the site feel more educational/formal or more community/social?
3. Do you want the homepage at `/` to be one selected design, or a gateway page that links to `/1` to `/5`?

## Brand and Voice
1. Do you already have a brand name and slogan?
2. Preferred tone: serious and factual, energetic and youth-focused, or mixed?
3. Do you want anonymous testimonials highlighted or de-emphasized?

## Content Model
1. Which fields are mandatory in every job submission?
2. Should salary be required, optional, or hidden by default?
3. Do you want contributors to upload photos, or text-only at launch?
4. Should users be allowed to submit without login in v1?

## Moderation and Trust
1. Do you want all submissions manually approved before publishing?
2. Should rejected submissions notify contributors (if contact is provided)?
3. Any legal restrictions for age or region we should enforce at launch?

## Design Preferences
1. Which two of the five concepts should be highest fidelity first?
2. Any colors to avoid or definitely include?
3. Any visual references you like (sites, posters, magazines, apps)?
4. Motion preference: subtle, medium, or bold animations?

## Technical Constraints
1. Are you comfortable using Supabase free tier for database/auth?
2. Do you want to keep v1 static-first and add DB integration after designs are approved?
3. Should we support only English first, or plan multilingual structure now?

## Launch and Operations
1. Target launch date?
2. Do you want basic analytics from day one?
3. Who will moderate submissions regularly?
4. Do you want a simple admin page in v1 or manual DB moderation first?

## Decision Checklist
Please provide at least:
1. Preferred route behavior for `/`.
2. Database choice for v1.
3. Top two homepage concepts to prioritize.
4. Must-have submission fields.
5. Target launch timeline.
